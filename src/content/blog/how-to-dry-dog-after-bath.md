Drying your dog properly after a bath is just as important as the bath itself. Improper drying can lead to skin issues, matting, and discomfort.

## Why Proper Drying Matters

### Skin Health
- Trapped moisture causes bacterial and yeast growth
- Can lead to hot spots
- Causes skin irritation
- Creates unpleasant odor

### Coat Health
- Wet fur mats more easily
- Air drying can cause tangles
- Curly coats especially vulnerable
- Proper drying maintains coat texture

## Drying Methods

### Towel Drying

**Best for**: Starting the drying process, short-coated breeds

**Technique**:
- Use absorbent microfiber towels
- Press and squeezeâ€”don't rub
- Rubbing causes tangles and breaks hair
- Change towels when saturated
- Focus on getting excess water out

### Air Drying

**Best for**: Short-coated breeds in warm weather only

**Cautions**:
- Never for thick or long coats
- Can cause matting in curly coats
- Keep dog warm until dry
- Not recommended in cold weather

### Blow Dryer

**Best for**: Most dogs, essential for thick and long coats

**Types**:
- **Human hair dryers**: Use on cool or low heat only
- **High-velocity dryers**: Professional tool, no heat needed
- **Pet-specific dryers**: Designed for dog coats

## Step-by-Step Drying Guide

### 1. Remove Excess Water
- Squeeze water from fur
- Use absorbent towels
- Work from head to tail

### 2. Protect Ears
- Dry ears thoroughly
- Use cotton balls to absorb moisture
- Prevents ear infections

### 3. Start Drying
- Work in sections
- Keep dryer moving
- Maintain safe distance
- Check temperature on your hand

### 4. Brush While Drying
- Helps prevent tangles
- Speeds up drying
- Ensures thorough drying
- Results in fluffy, smooth coat

### 5. Check Trouble Spots
- Under ears
- Belly
- Armpits
- Between toes
- Under tail

## Safety Tips

### Avoid Burns
- Never use high heat
- Keep dryer moving
- Test temperature frequently
- Watch for signs of discomfort

### Reduce Stress
- Introduce dryer gradually
- Use treats and praise
- Keep sessions short initially
- Consider a dryer with low noise

## Common Drying Mistakes

1. **Rubbing with towels** - causes tangles
2. **Using high heat** - burns skin
3. **Not drying completely** - causes skin issues
4. **Skipping hard-to-reach areas** - leads to problems
5. **Air drying long coats** - causes matting

## By Coat Type

### Short Coats
- Towel dry thoroughly
- Air dry may be acceptable
- Quick brush when dry

### Double Coats
- Towel dry well
- Use blow dryer on cool
- Dry undercoat completely
- Brush while drying

### Long/Silky Coats
- Gentle towel pressing
- Blow dry on low heat
- Brush continuously while drying
- Use conditioning spray

### Curly Coats
- Towel press gently
- Blow dry on cool
- Brush while drying (crucial!)
- Air drying causes severe matting
